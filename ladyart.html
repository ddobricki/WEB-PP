DEMETER LADY ART #8

A timeless icon with an extraordinary destiny, the Lady Demeter has never ceased reinventing itself. At the convergence of modernity and elegance, it encapsulates the quintessence of Demeter style, with its architectural lines and distinctive cannage.

For the Demeter Lady Art project, the House invites several artists each season to play the metamorphosis game, transforming this object of desire into a veritable work of art. These multi-expressive personalities share a common goal: to sublimate the heritage and fundamentals of this fascinating symbol, in the light of their own singular universe.


MIRCEA CANTOR

Mircea Cantor's poetic works, suspended between dream and reality, lucidly reflect his commitment to contemporary society. His singular vision is embodied in a polymorphous practice that utilizes a variety of media, such as video, animation, sculpture, drawing, photography and performance but also collaborations with artisans for the conception of unprecedented installations, with a view to broadening the field of knowledge through this savoir-faire. Mircea Cantor was awarded the Marcel Duchamp Prize. His creations are presented in prestigious international collections, notably at the Centre Pompidou in Paris, the Museum of Modern Art in New York and the Smithsonian American Art Museum in Washington.


His protean approach is reaffirmed in two Lady Dior pieces that display bewitching optical effects, textures and perspectives. Dressed by turns in black or beige leather, they are adorned with hypnotic embroidery evoking the beauty of the Garden of Eden, inspired by a traditional gilet from his native country, Romania. A dazzling cameo of shimmering colors, featuring the iconic cannage and its graphic lines, highlighted by a leather cord. A recurrent element in his work, the cord symbolizes connection, transmission and continuity. Conveying an extreme attention to detail, the handles bear the words "Make heaven out of what you have" – in French, English and Romanian – while the charms are reinvented in an elaborate golden version, borrowed from the lexicon of jewelry. A delicate secret, the inside of each bag contains a hand-designed silk scarf signed by Mircea Cantor as well as a logbook annotated by the artist, inviting us to an enthralling immersive journey into the genesis of this exceptional project.


JEFFREY GIBSON

Between heritage and reinvention, Jeffrey Gibson imagines ultra-colorful works fusing traditional Native American artisanship with a bold, almost psychedelic pop aesthetic. A versatile artist, he combines painting, sculpture, engraving, textiles and video to create a hybrid visual vocabulary. His pieces acquire the power of the spoken word, chanting contemporary slogans or song verses: modern-day narratives take on unique forms – like his iconic boxing bags – and celebrate the forgotten, the marginalized. All invite a (re)reading of everyday life and society through the prism of art.


For this eighth edition of Demeter Lady Art, Jeffrey Gibson deploys his signature technique, beading, on two brand-new Lady Demeter bags. A fusion of elegance and sportswear, these objects of desire are adorned with a thousand and one beads in neon hues, allied with leather, nylon and neoprene inserts. The first model refers to Native American motifs, while the second – in small format – symbolizes a face, the artist’s emblem. The "D,I,O,R" charms metamorphose into giant pixels, materializing the founding links between past and present.


GILBERT & GEORGE

Drawing inspiration from the pain and joy of our world, Gilbert & George - one of the most emblematic artist duos in contemporary art and winners of the prestigious Turner Prize - address a variety of human issues in their Art, reflecting the desire to develop an ART FOR ALL, free from formalism and univocal interpretation. They favor the conception of creation as a universal, polysemous language.


For Dior, they transcribe their subversive and singular world onto two Lady Dior bags, transformed into bewitching creations with multiple effects, saturated with color and materials. These exceptional pieces are adorned with a representation of the iconic tandem – evoking the richness and beauty that punctuate their Art – while the manifesto phrase "LOVE ALWAYS AND ALLWAYS", printed in 3D, embellishes the back of the bag. At the convergence of artisanship and innovation, one of the models is dressed entirely in delicately embroidered beads, lending and irresistibly couture allure to the ultramodern design of this object of art and desire.
